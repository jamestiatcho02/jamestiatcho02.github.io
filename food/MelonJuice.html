Melon Juice

Melon juice made of shredded cantaloupe and simple syrup is a refreshing drink you’ll love year round. Bursting with fresh fruit flavor, it’s a tasty and healthy way to hydrate!

Ingredients


1 small cantaloupe

3/4 to 1 cup granulated white sugar

10 to 12 cups water

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Ice cubes

Procedures


Procedure #1

Slice the cantaloupe into half. Remove all the seeds.


Procedure #2

Using a melon baller or special cantaloupe scraper, scrape the cantaloupe and place in a large bowl. Set aside.


Procedure #3

Use a cookie scoop to portion out the cake mixture. Then, Roll the mixture into 1-inch balls.


Procedure #4

In a large pitcher, combine water, sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir until the sugar is diluted. Add the shredded cantaloupe. Stir again.


Procedure #5

Put lots of ice. Serve, share and enjoy!

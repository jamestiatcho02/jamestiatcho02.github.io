Egg Mayo Sandwich

Egg mayo sandwich recipe is proof of how good a classic sandwich and boiled egg can be. It is loaded with flavour, soft, creamy and downright tasty.

Ingredients


3 hard-boiled eggs, cut into small cubes

3/4 cup mayonnaise

12 slices white bread

3 bunches of curly leaf lettuce, washed and dried from excess water (optional)

2 pcs ripe salad tomatoes, sliced (optional)

Procedures


Procedure #1

Procedure #2

To add a healthy twist to your Classic Egg Sandwich, you can arrange 3-4 pcs of lettuce leaves on the bread then spread mixture over the lettuce, top with 1-2 slices of tomatoes then cover with another slice. Enjoy with a glass of milk and fruit slices!
